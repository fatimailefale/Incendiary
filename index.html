About Incendiary

"Incendiary: person who excites factions, quarrels, or sedition"

Created in late 2022 Incendiary is a resell retailer with quality clothes that come from a loved home to you!

At cheap and affordable prices for any and all our goal is to responsibly recycle clothing into happy customers homes. Launched in Wellington New Zealand we do international trading and shipping - opening the wonderful closet to people across the world.

Founder Fatimai Lefale looked to sell clothes in an ethical and well managed way to those who wanted well kept second hand. This is what Incendiary delievers on with every package sent your way.

Check out Incendiary's clothing, here at HQ we know you'll find something perfect!
